About The Owner/President of Strategic One Consulting, LLC

Shamisa Ingram, MA

Shamisa Ingram has several years of experience writing proposals and grant applications. Since 2008, Ms. Ingram has developed unique business writing strategies for developing successful proposals; grant applications, articles, and marketing email blast campaigns. Shamisa has a Bachelor of Arts degree in English from Spelman College and a Master of Arts in Sport Management from the University of San Francisco. From 2008-2011, Ms. Ingram worked for a private forensic DNA laboratory as the Marketing and Research Coordinator. In her role as a Marketing and Research Coordinator, Shamisa work numerous successful proposals to include; Request for Proposals, Request for Qualifications, Request for Information, and Request for Bids. Her strategic approach to business writing facilitated winning proposals with awards totally over $800,000. Additionally, she has assisted many non-profit organizations with grant applications (awards totally over $300,000). Furthermore, she has developed informative articles and email blast campaign to boost the marketing efforts of companies. She also has experience conducting research using an in-depth quantitative and qualitative approach to substantiated key market trends. Based on research findings, she was able to persuade the executive management team of a company to take a strategic approach to reach prospective clients.

Ms. Ingram has received continuing education in the following areas; Grant Writing (Certificate of Completion of Grant Writing from the Salt Lake City Community College and the Utah Art Council), and Technical Communications (Graduate Certificate program at Illinois Institute of Technology). Additionally, Shamisa has a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt Certification. Ms. Ingram's educational background and unique work experience has equipped her with indispensible skills to provide exceptional consultative services in the areas of business writing. Her passion for assisting business in their growth and development inspired her to start Strategic One Consulting, LLC; a consulting company that provides strategic solutions for successful business writing applications.
